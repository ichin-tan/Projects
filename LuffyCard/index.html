About the character

Monkey D. Luffy, also known as "Straw Hat" Luffy, is a fictional character and the protagonist in the Japanese manga series One Piece created by Eiichiro Oda. He fearlessly pursues the legendary treasure of the late Gol D. Roger in order to become the new Pirate King. He believes that being the Pirate King means having the most freedom in the world. He fights multiple antagonists, and aids and befriends the inhabitants of several islands on his journey.

Luffy is best known for:

  1. Bounty: Luffy currently has one of the highest bounties in the entire series, set at 3 billion berries, surpassed only by characters like Shanks, Mihawk, and Blackbeard.
  2. Devil Fruit powers: Luffy is known for his Devil Fruit powers. He ate the Gomu Gomu no Mi, which gave him a rubber-like body, allowing him to stretch and enhance his physical abilities
  3. Haki: Luffy has mastered all three types of Haki: Observation Haki to sense others and glimpse the future, Armament Haki to strengthen his attacks, and the rare Conqueror's Haki to overpower weaker opponents.
